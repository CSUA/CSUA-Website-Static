Industry

  • The Berkeley CSUA works closely with entrepreneurs, companies, investors, and active members of the software industry to bring both mentorship and job opportunities to our students.

    Interested in working with the CSUA to sponsor an event or to speak at one of our eTalks events? Send us an email at politburo@csuaberkeley.edu, and we’ll get back to you within 24 hours.

Some of Our Biggest Sponsors

The CSUA is dedicated to improving the experience of computer science undergraduates.

Meraki: Meraki brings the benefits of the cloud to edge and branch networks, delivering easy-to-manage wireless, switching, and security solutions that enable customers to seize new business opportunities and reduce operational cost. Meraki is the primary sponsor of our Spring 2013 Hackathon.

Ness: Ness, founded by CSUA alumni, cosponsored our Spring 2012 Hackathon and is a great supporter of the CSUA.

Heroku: Heroku cosponsored our Spring 2012 Hackathon and provides a wonderful development platform that many of our members routinely use in their hacking endeavors.

Palantir Technologies: Palantir has long been an incredible supporter of the Berkeley CSUA, helping us with everything from running workshops to going on paintball outings.

Yahoo: Every fall, we team up with Yahoo to organize our Hack Day event. We give out free food, awesome prizes, and bring together UC Berkeley’s greatest programmers to build cool stuff for a weekend.

SAY Media: A modern media company connects paid and earned media via an integrated brand-marketing platform that yields meaningful and measurable returns. It’s the only accountable way to engage social consumers at scale.

Alsop Louie: Alsop Louie Partners is a partnership between Stewart Alsop and Gilman Louie designed to provide venture capital to the most promising entrepreneurs we can find. It is structured as a traditional limited partnership, where the partnership between Stewart and Gilman manages the investments of the firm and the investors are the limited partners.

Eat Metrics: Eat Metrics builds analytics and advertising solutions for restaurants. With more accurate analytics, restaurants can target, acquire and track their customers from the web, to the restaurant.

Qualcomm: Qualcomm’s technology connects you where you are in the world. We manufacture chip sets, license technology, and provide communications services around the world. In a global economy, we are the link to markets and opportunity.
